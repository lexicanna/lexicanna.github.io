Marijuana Information Services

Lexicanna would like to help you through Oregon’s Recreational Marijuana Licensing process. While helping you, we hope to increase our own knowledge of how we can further help small marijuana businesses in our community.

Our long term goal is to use our business writing and information technology skills to develop products for the legal marijuana industry. Like you, we are learning about Oregon’s state and local requirements for licensure as they evolve. We will stay abreast of these rules and regulations, so that we can help small business people like you can be compliant and have a good chance at licensure.

Beginning in January we will be offering A License for Mary Jane workshops around the Rogue Valley. In these workshops we will present strategies which can assist you to get your Oregon Recreational Marijuana license. We also expect to learn from you. Your concerns, experiences, and needs concerning your adventure of becoming a legal marijuana business are important to us. The more we know about this journey to legalization, the better we will be able to help you and others.

A License for Mary Jane Workshops

Strategies to Build Your Legal Marijuana Business

A preliminary list of topics to be covered in these workshops include navigating local, state, and federal regulations; accumulating background work to develop and evolve your business; learning what specific information will help you obtain your license; maintaining license compliance; and locating helpful local resources. If you have suggestions of other topics we would appreciate hearing from you. For further details about what this workshop will cover go to workshops.
